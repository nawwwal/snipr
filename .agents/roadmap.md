# snipr roadmap

This file is intentionally high level.

Plane is the source of truth for roadmap phases, feature sequencing, milestones, and work items. Use the Plane project `snipr` (`SNIP`) for the active plan.

## What snipr is becoming

`snipr` is becoming an extension-first, agent-ready visual capture tool.

It should let a user click a Chrome extension from the page they are already on, capture the current browser context, and turn that context into an annotated image, video storyboard, recording, or agent-ready export.

## Why this exists

Agents are still bad at consuming raw visual context, especially long videos and messy capture workflows.

`snipr` exists to close that gap:

- capture the current browser moment quickly
- detect supported page context, starting with X/Twitter video posts
- turn motion into agent-readable artifacts such as storyboards and keyframes
- annotate the important parts
- copy or share the result with minimal friction

## Product pillars

1. Extension-first capture: detect the active page and launch the right capture workflow.
2. Video to storyboard: keep the current video storyboarding flow and make it work from the active tab.
3. Screenshot and recording modes: capture, annotate, copy, download, or storyboard browser evidence.
4. Convert for agents: exports, metadata, notes, and prompt-ready bundles.
